# Synthetic Data Generation Using Large Language Models

- **Authors**: Mihai Nadas, Laura Diosan, Andreea Tomescu
- **Published**: Mar 2025
- **Venue**: IEEE Access 13 (2025)

## One-Liner
LLM-driven synthetic data for text/code: prompt-based, retrieval-augmented, iterative self-refinement. Covers benefits, challenges, and mitigation strategies.
