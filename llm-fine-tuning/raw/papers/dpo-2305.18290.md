# DPO: Your Language Model is Secretly a Reward Model

- **Authors**: Rafael Rafailov, Archit Sharma, Eric Mitchell, Stefano Ermon, Christopher D. Manning, Chelsea Finn
- **Published**: May 2023 (v3: Jul 2024)
- **Venue**: NeurIPS 2023
- **License**: CC BY 4.0

## One-Liner
Re-parameterizes RLHF reward model to extract optimal policy in closed form — reduces preference alignment to a simple classification loss, no RL needed.

## Key Results
- Exceeds PPO-based RLHF on sentiment control
- Matches or improves summarization and single-turn dialogue
- Substantially simpler to implement and train
